Sink the Pink

Brand identity

Part of East London's underground scene since 2009, these well established club promoters have nurtured and supported a growing scene to include performances, club nights and international collaborations.

Create a fresh and recognisable logo to feature on promotional materials, stage sets and merchandise, reflecting a self proclaimed style as vibrant, trashy, fun, frivolous and unapologetic.

The logo I produced relied heavily on colourful neon characters synonymous with night clubs, with a street level aesthetic reflecting the diversity of the promoters, the club scene and their followers.

The resulting uniqueness of the logo and its eclectic characters has proved versatile, working well as a stand alone brand marker, whilst allowing for animation in digital formats, and multiple on-stage construction methods. The logo can be seen at large scale events and performances such The Mioght Hoopla, London's Troxy, Bestival, Glastonbury and Selfridges.
